ZeroProof

I love a good cocktail. I love making them, drinking them, and talking about their histories and construction. Sitting with friends around the dinner table after a delicious meal, sipping on a drink mixed to my preferences with a complex flavor and sexy garnish, all while discussing how I designed the drink or where a particular ingredient came from can make a simple meal among friends turn into a big night out.

An affair.

But as I grow older, I find myself and my friends looking for ways to achieve that moment without the price of a bad morning or a ride share home. This is what inspired me to create a Mocktail app. As more and more people go searching for a nonalcoholic cocktail alternative, whether its due to health concerns or a matter of taste, I was finding it difficult to find recipes that could strike a balance between an interesting drink and an absence of alcohol. I wanted to create a space where anyone looking for a nonalcoholic alternative could still find something classy, mature, and interesting.

The Final Product

I built out the final product for 4 screen sizes- a desk top site, a tablet, and 2 smaller mobile devices. The clean, soft, and inviting look I was hoping for was coming through and the site was ready to open itself up to users and help them begin their journey into mocktail mixology.

Feedback so far has been glowing. Both for the product and the design.

Users have been excited by the prospect of an app they can depend on to provide a fun, classy experience without the worries that may come with alcohol consumption. Moving forward, I hope to expand the apps libraries and begin looking into other mediums for connecting with users- perhaps a YouTube channel, some type of physical publication, and so on.

I am excited to continue my journey with ZeroProof.
