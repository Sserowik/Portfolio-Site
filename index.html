My Work

MixTape

Using Adobe XD, I created a design for a retro music app that focused on creating digital mixtapes to share. MixTape was one of my earliest designs and still holds a special place for me, using some of my favorite colors, fonts, and layouts I've yet to work with. I created flows for users to find music, share music, and- most importantly- design their own tapes to show off to other users.

ZeroProof

ZeroProof is a mocktail recipe and research site that I designed. I used Adobe XD to create 4 different formats for screens ranging everywhere from the smallest mobile screens to the largest desktops. It features pages for recipes, articles, and a glossary feature to help users learn and create non-alcoholic drinks that can rival their favorite boozy beverage.

A Seat at the Tabletop

A Seat at the Tabletop is an app I designed for mobile devices to help users locate local tabletop games looking for players. I used adobe XD and Figma to help bring A Seat at the Tabletop to life.

ThreadCraft

Using primarily Figma, I designed threadCraft as a mobile based shopping app that focuses on providing unique sizing considerations for users. The style of the clothing, and subsequently the app, has a Nerdcore focus but the true hook of the app is the filtering options that allow users to look for clothes that are gender free, disability friendly, and overall more considerate of the varying bodies that exist in the world.
